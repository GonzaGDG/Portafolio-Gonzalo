Team Coordination in a Competitive Multiplayer Environment

In 2023, I took on the leadership of a Lineage 2 multiplayer clan composed of 40 to 80 players from Chile and Argentina. Over the course of a year, I coordinated daily operations: team building, task assignment, resource distribution, and strategy development aimed at ensuring balanced progression and achieving competitive advantages.

This experience was key to strengthening my leadership, real-time decision-making, and people management skills in a fast-paced, multicultural, and high-pressure environment.
